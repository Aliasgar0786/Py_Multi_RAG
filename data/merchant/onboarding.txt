Shopcard Merchant Onboarding Guide

Getting Started as a Seller:
To become a Shopcard merchant, submit an application through the Seller Portal. You will need to provide business information including business name, registration number, tax ID, and bank account details for payouts.

Application Review:
Applications are reviewed within 3-5 business days. You will receive an email notification when your application is approved. If additional information is needed, a Shopcard representative will contact you.

Seller Tiers:
- Basic Seller: New merchants with limited listing capacity (up to 100 products). Commission rate: 12%.
- Verified Seller: Merchants with verified business documents. Up to 1,000 products. Commission rate: 10%.
- Premium Seller: High-volume merchants with excellent performance metrics. Unlimited products. Commission rate: 8%.
- Enterprise Seller: Custom terms for large-scale operations. Contact sales for details.

Account Setup:
After approval, configure your seller profile by adding a store name, logo, description, and return policy. Set up your shipping preferences including shipping zones, rates, and estimated delivery times.

Compliance Requirements:
All merchants must comply with Shopcard's Seller Agreement, including product safety standards, accurate product descriptions, timely shipping, and responsive customer service. Violations may result in account suspension.

Payout Schedule:
Payouts are processed bi-weekly on the 1st and 15th of each month. Minimum payout threshold is $25. Funds are transferred to the bank account registered during onboarding.
