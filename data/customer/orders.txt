Shopcard Consumer Order Guide

Placing an Order:
To place an order on Shopcard, browse products through categories or search. Add items to your cart by clicking "Add to Cart." When ready, proceed to checkout. You can review your items, apply discount codes, and select your preferred shipping method.

Order Tracking:
After placing an order, you will receive a confirmation email with your order number. To track your order, go to "My Orders" in your account. Each order shows real-time status updates: Processing, Shipped, Out for Delivery, and Delivered.

Shipping Options:
- Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (free for orders over $50).
- Express Shipping: 2-3 business days ($9.99).
- Same-Day Delivery: Available in select cities ($14.99).

Order Cancellation:
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement if they haven't entered the processing stage. After processing begins, cancellation is no longer possible, but you can request a return after delivery.

Order Issues:
If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact Shopcard support within 48 hours. Provide photos of the issue and your order number. We will arrange a replacement or refund.

Payment Methods:
Shopcard accepts credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shopcard Gift Cards.
