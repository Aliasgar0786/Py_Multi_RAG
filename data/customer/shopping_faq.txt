Shopcard Consumer Shopping Guide and FAQs

Creating an Account:
To shop on Shopcard, create a free account using your email address or sign up with Google or Apple. You can also browse as a guest, but an account is required to place orders and track purchases.

Product Search and Discovery:
Use the search bar to find products by name, brand, or category. Filter results by price range, rating, shipping speed, and availability. Save items to your wishlist for later.

Product Reviews:
Read verified buyer reviews before purchasing. Each product page shows an average rating and detailed reviews. You can sort reviews by most helpful, most recent, or by rating.

Deals and Discounts:
Check the "Deals" section for daily offers and flash sales. Subscribe to the Shopcard newsletter for exclusive discount codes. Use the price comparison tool to ensure you're getting the best deal.

Account Management:
Manage your profile, addresses, and payment methods from the Account Settings page. You can add multiple shipping addresses and set a default. Enable two-factor authentication for added security.

Customer Support:
Contact Shopcard customer support through the Help Center, live chat, email, or phone. Support hours are 8 AM to 10 PM EST, Monday through Saturday. For urgent issues, use the live chat for the fastest response.

Privacy and Data:
Shopcard respects your privacy. You can view, download, or delete your personal data at any time from Account Settings. We never sell personal data to third parties.
