Shopcard Consumer Refund Policy

How Refunds Work:
When a consumer is not satisfied with a purchase, they can request a refund within 30 days of delivery. To initiate a refund, go to your order history, select the order, and click "Request Refund." You will need to provide a reason for the refund request.

Refund Eligibility:
- Items must be in original condition and packaging.
- Digital products are non-refundable once downloaded or activated.
- Perishable goods are non-refundable unless they arrived damaged.
- Custom or personalized items cannot be refunded.

Refund Processing Time:
- Refund requests are reviewed within 2-3 business days.
- Once approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
- Refunds are returned to the original payment method.
- If paid by credit card, the refund may take an additional 1-2 billing cycles to appear.

Partial Refunds:
- Partial refunds may be issued for items that show signs of use.
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a seller error.

Refund Disputes:
If a refund request is denied, consumers can escalate the dispute through the Shopcard Resolution Center. A dedicated team will review the case and provide a final decision within 5 business days.
