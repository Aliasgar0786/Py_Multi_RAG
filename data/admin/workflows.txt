Shopcard Admin Workflow Guide

Merchant Application Review Workflow:
1. New merchant applications appear in the "Pending Applications" queue.
2. Verify business documentation (registration, tax ID, bank details).
3. Conduct a background check using the integrated verification service.
4. Approve or reject the application with a documented reason.
5. For approved merchants, trigger the automated onboarding email sequence.
6. For rejected applications, send a rejection notice with improvement suggestions.

Product Review Workflow:
1. New product listings enter the "Pending Review" queue.
2. Check product images for quality and compliance.
3. Verify product description accuracy and completeness.
4. Ensure pricing is within acceptable ranges for the category.
5. Approve, reject, or request modifications.
6. Communicate decisions to the merchant within 24 hours.

Escalated Support Workflow:
1. Escalated tickets from Consumer or Merchant support teams appear in the Admin queue.
2. Review the full ticket history and previous resolution attempts.
3. Contact involved parties if additional information is needed.
4. Make a resolution decision based on platform policies.
5. Implement the resolution (refund, credit, account action).
6. Update the ticket with the resolution and close it.

Promotional Campaign Workflow:
1. Marketing team submits a campaign request with details and budget.
2. Admin reviews the campaign for compliance with platform policies.
3. Configure the campaign in the Promotions Management tool.
4. Schedule the campaign with start and end dates.
5. Monitor campaign performance during the active period.
6. Generate a post-campaign report with results and ROI analysis.

Data Export and Reporting Workflow:
1. Receive data export or reporting requests from stakeholders.
2. Verify the requester has appropriate authorization.
3. Generate the requested report from the Reporting Dashboard.
4. Review the report for data accuracy and sensitivity.
5. Deliver the report through the secure document portal.
6. Log the export in the Data Access Register for compliance.
