Shopcard Admin Platform Procedures

User Management:
Admins can manage user accounts across all platforms (Consumer, Merchant, Admin). Actions include: viewing user profiles, suspending or banning accounts, resetting passwords, and reviewing account activity logs.

Account Suspension Process:
1. Review the reported violation or flagged activity.
2. Verify the violation with supporting evidence.
3. Issue a warning for first-time minor violations.
4. Suspend the account for repeated or major violations.
5. Document the action in the Admin Activity Log.
6. Notify the affected user via email with the reason and appeal process.

Content Moderation:
Review flagged product listings, reviews, and messages. Remove content that violates Shopcard's Community Guidelines. Categories of violations include: counterfeit products, misleading descriptions, hate speech, and spam.

Dispute Resolution:
Handle escalated disputes between consumers and merchants. Review transaction history, communication logs, and evidence from both parties. Issue a resolution decision and communicate it to all parties within 5 business days.

Platform Configuration:
Manage global platform settings including: commission rates, shipping policies, payment gateway configurations, and promotional campaigns. Changes to platform-wide settings require approval from a Senior Admin.

Compliance and Auditing:
Conduct regular audits of merchant accounts, transaction records, and platform operations. Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements. Generate compliance reports for internal review and external audits.
